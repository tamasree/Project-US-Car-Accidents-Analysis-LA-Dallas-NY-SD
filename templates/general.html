Accident Counts Trend For Cities


Across the four cities, total accident counts in Los Angeles and San Diego is showing high in one year from July 2019. Other two cities remain the same trend. Accident counts drops down during July 2020 and increases almost twice higher towards the end of December 2020. The drop down is significantly influenced by city's lock down mandatory due to Covid-19 pandemic.

Accident Counts Variation for Different Factors


We analyzed accident counts and different factors. The first bar chart shows total accident counts and traffic conditions and it shows most accident occurred on the Junctions and where traffic signals are existing.


The weather conditions analysis shows top accident occurrence are under fair weather conditions.

Accident Serverity Analysis


Plot Type

The accident events are catagorize into four levels, ranking from 1 to 4. The plot shows that most of the accidents had average severity or level 2, then followed by level 3 or above average.

We looked at how other factors like windspeed (mph) and precipitation (in) would effect the severity of the accident. The plots show low correlations between severity with both factors
